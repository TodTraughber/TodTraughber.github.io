The FIRST Robotics Competition is a high-tech sporting event that aligns robots against each other in a fierce competition. In addition to the machines participating in the competition, student drivers, cheering fans, parents and referees give the event the feel of an intense sporting event.

During the competitions, judges work together to identify the robots that deserve recognition. By going out into the pits (where the robots are kept and worked on between matches) and interviewing the students, judges are an important part of what makes an FRC event so special.
