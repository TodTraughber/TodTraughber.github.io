Review: Awards Based Upon the Machine:

Industrial Design Award

Celebrates form and function in an efficiently designed machine that effectively addresses the game challenge.
Key phrases:
The design is elegant and efficient • Designing the machine contributes to the team’s success in FIRST Robotics • Reliability and maintainability are considered in design •The entire machine design, or the detailed process used to develop the design, is worthy of this recognition, and not just a single component.

Quality Award

Celebrates machine robustness in concept and fabrication.
Key phrases:
The entire team demonstrates robustness and quality • The machine can withstand the rigors of competition • Workmanship is valued and planned in both the machine and support equipment • Execution is superb; the machine was built with a detailed plan in mind.

Excellence in Engineering Award

Celebrates an elegant and advantageous machine feature.
Key phrases:
A team spokesperson must be able to competently describe the engineered feature(s) and can trace its conception, design, manufacturing/assembly, or deployment • The feature(s) reflect an engineering solution to a specific problem, and it is functional and practical • The feature(s) are elegant and advantageous on the field of play • It stands up to the rigors of competition.
