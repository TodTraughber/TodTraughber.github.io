Awards Based Upon Team Attributes:

Engineering Inspiration Award

Celebrates outstanding success in advancing respect and appreciation for engineering within a team’s school and community.

Guidelines:

  • Extent and inventiveness of the team’s efforts to recruit students to engineering with particular emphasis on the most recent year’s efforts. There is measurable success of those efforts.

  • Extent and effectiveness of the team’s community outreach efforts with particular emphasis on the most recent year’s efforts.

  • A commitment to science and technology education among the team, school, and community.

  • Achievement of the FIRST mission and ability to communicate that at the competition and away from it.

THE WINNER OF THIS AWARD AUTOMATICALLY QUALIFIES FOR THE WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS!!
