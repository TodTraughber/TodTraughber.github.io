Review: Awards Based Upon Team Attributes:

Engineering Inspiration Award

Celebrates outstanding success in advancing respect and appreciation for engineering within a team’s school and community.
Key phrases:
Extent and inventiveness of the team’s efforts to recruit students to engineering with particular emphasis on the most recent year’s efforts • Extent and effectiveness of the team’s community outreach efforts • A commitment to science and technology education among the team, school, and community • Achievement of the FIRST mission and ability to communicate that at the competition and away from it.
